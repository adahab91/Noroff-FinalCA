How to defeat your opponent

Each players starts the game with 20 life. Knock your opponent down to 0 life, and you win. The most common way to do this is to summon creatures and attack with them.

Creature cards are the most important of many Something decks. Theyre really easy to spot--just look at the lower right of a card. If you see a pair of numbers seperated by a stash youve got a creature card. Once its on the battlefield, a creature continues to attack and defend for you until your opponent can find a way to take it out.

Most games become a race to see who can deal with most damage first. Summoning the best creatures would help you win that race every time. Check out the section on casting creature spells.
